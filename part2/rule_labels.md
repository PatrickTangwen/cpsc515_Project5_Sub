# Rule Labels For Unsat Core Explanations

These labels are the ones we want to see in unsat cores and later turn into legal explanations.
`rule_152_b2_textual_strict`
Statute: §152(b)(2)
Meaning: Strict textual version: a joint return bars dependent treatment.

`rule_152_b2_joint_return`
Statute: §152(b)(2), harmonized with §152(c)(1)(E)
Meaning: Final Project 4 style rule: a refund-only joint return does not disqualify the individual.

`rule_152_b3_general_residency`
Statute: §152(b)(3)(A)
Meaning: The individual must be a U.S. citizen/national, U.S. resident, or resident of a contiguous country.

`rule_152_b3_adopted_child_exception`
Statute: §152(b)(3)(B)
Meaning: Adopted-child exception to the general citizenship/residency rule.

`rule_152_b3_citizenship_or_residency`
Statute: §152(b)(3)
Meaning: Final citizenship/residency gate for dependent status.

`rule_152_c1A_qc_relationship`
Statute: §152(c)(1)(A), §152(c)(2)
Meaning: Qualifying-child relationship test.

`rule_152_c1B_qc_same_abode`
Statute: §152(c)(1)(B)
Meaning: Qualifying child must share the same principal place of abode for more than half the year.

`rule_152_c1C_qc_age`
Statute: §152(c)(1)(C), §152(c)(3)
Meaning: Qualifying-child age or disability rule.

`rule_152_c1D_qc_self_support`
Statute: §152(c)(1)(D)
Meaning: Individual must not provide more than half of their own support.

`rule_152_c1E_qc_joint_return`
Statute: §152(c)(1)(E)
Meaning: Qualifying-child joint-return rule, with refund-only carveout.

`rule_152_c1_qc_candidate_definition`
Statute: §152(c)(1)
Meaning: Combines the five qualifying-child candidate conditions.

`rule_152_c4_qc_tiebreaker`
Statute: §152(c)(4)
Meaning: Tie-breaker among competing qualifying-child claimants.

`rule_152_c_qc_definition`
Statute: §152(c)
Meaning: Final qualifying-child definition.

`rule_152_d2_qr_relationship`
Statute: §152(d)(2)
Meaning: Qualifying-relative relationship or household-member test.

`rule_152_d4_effective_gross_income`
Statute: §152(d)(4)
Meaning: Sheltered-workshop income exclusion when computing gross income.

`rule_152_d1B_qr_gross_income`
Statute: §152(d)(1)(B)
Meaning: Gross income must be below the exemption amount.

`rule_152_d5A_effective_support`
Statute: §152(d)(5)(A)
Meaning: Support adjustments for alimony and support from a new spouse.

`rule_152_d1C_qr_support_direct`
Statute: §152(d)(1)(C)
Meaning: Direct support rule: taxpayer provides over half of support.

`rule_152_d3_qr_multiple_support`
Statute: §152(d)(3)
Meaning: Multiple-support agreement alternative.

`rule_152_d1C_qr_support`
Statute: §152(d)(1)(C) and §152(d)(3)
Meaning: Final support rule for qualifying-relative status.

`rule_152_d1D_qr_not_qc_of_anyone`
Statute: §152(d)(1)(D)
Meaning: Individual must not be a qualifying child of the taxpayer or any other taxpayer.

`rule_152_d_qr_definition`
Statute: §152(d)
Meaning: Final qualifying-relative definition.

`rule_152_a_dependent_definition`
Statute: §152(a)
Meaning: Final dependent definition.

`rule_152_b1_no_dependents_of_own`
Statute: §152(b)(1)
Meaning: Dependent cannot have dependents of their own in the same calendar year.
